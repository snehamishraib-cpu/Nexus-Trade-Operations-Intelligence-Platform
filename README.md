# 📊 Nexus Trade Operations Intelligence Platform

## Executive Overview

The Nexus Trade Operations Intelligence Platform is an end-to-end Power BI dashboard developed to simulate real-world Investment Banking Trade Operations.

The project provides operational visibility across the complete trade lifecycle including trade monitoring, settlement performance, exception management, operational action tracking and executive reporting.

This solution is designed to support Operations Managers, Team Leads and Executive Management in monitoring operational efficiency, reducing settlement risk and identifying process bottlenecks.

---

# Business Problem

Trade Operations teams process thousands of financial transactions every day.

Operational challenges include:

- Failed Settlements
- Trade Exceptions
- Delayed Confirmations
- Manual Processing
- High Operational Risk
- Lack of Executive Visibility

The objective of this project is to centralize operational reporting into a single interactive dashboard.

---

# Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 – Executive Command Center

Executive level KPI dashboard providing overall operational health.

Includes:

- Total Trades
- Settlement Rate
- STP Rate
- Failed Trades
- Pending Confirmations
- Trade Volume Trends
- Product Distribution

---

### Page 2 – Trade Explorer

Operational trade monitoring dashboard.

Includes:

- Trade Details
- Trade Status
- Product Analysis
- Counterparty Analysis
- Settlement Tracking

### Page 3 – Exception Intelligence

Operational Risk dashboard.

Includes:

- Exception Aging
- Product-wise Exceptions
- Counterparty Exceptions

### Page 4 – Action Center

Operational workflow dashboard.

Includes:

- Pending Actions
- High Priority Exceptions
- SLA Monitoring
- Resolution Tracking

### Page 5 – Executive Insights

Management reporting dashboard.

Includes:

- Key Operational Insights
